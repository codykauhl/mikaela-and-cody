Our Story

2008 to Present

  • 2008-2011

    Our Beginnings

    Cody and Mikaela became friends while they were undergraduate students at Southern Illinois University of Edwardsville.

  • 2011-2013

    Different Cities

    Cody moved to Kansas City to complete his master’s degree in music composition, and Mikaela relocated to Morgantown, West Virginia to earn her master’s degree in vocal performance.

  • 2014

    A Commission

    Mikaela commissioned a piece from Cody for her final master’s recital, which they premiered in 2014 and continued to perform at new music festivals.

  • 2015-2016

    Collaboration

    As they traveled and collaborated, they slowly realized their growing connection. While they don’t have an official “date-iversary,” they made things OFFICIAL-official in February of 2016.

Our COVID-19 Plan

While we know that our love is stronger than anything, health always comes first! We are closely monitoring the current global health crisis and following the State of Illinois Coronavirus Response plan.

In order to celebrate on April 10th, Illinois will need to be in Phase 5 of the “Restore Illinois” plan (meaning no gathering restrictions). If it appears that we will not be in Phase 5 by that time, we will reschedule our wedding for a later date and notify all of our guests via phone and email as soon as possible. Same people, same place, different day!

In addition to state mandates, we plan to incorporate the following safety and sanitation measures to minimize the risk of illness:

  • Assisted buffet
  • Hand sanitizing stations

We know that the world looks a lot different than it did when we got engaged in December of 2019. While we would love for all of our guests to mark “yes,” we completely understand if the current health crisis limits your ability to join us in April.
